Character Design

Rebecca Amaterasu

Age: 21

Nation: Fourside

Rebecca is a Designer who always find inspiration in songs, UI and pretty much anything. However, she end up in a Parallel world of Another Earth where she can use magic known as PSI Power.

She fights an evil force to save the world. This Evil Force is known as “The Crazed Sign.” Crazed Sign are known to steal bus stop signs in order to spread chaos to the world. It is up to Rebecca to save the world.
