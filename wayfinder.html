Summery

The Wayfinder project is a team school project in RIT. Our client is Rochester Regional Health (RRH). They want us to create an Augmented Reality that can help both Patients and Coworkers to help navigate the Hospital in a simple way. I was in a team that deals with other language barriers.

This team project is also a team of two people.

Problem

Problem

The problem of the RRH (Rochester Regional Health) Hospital is that people do get lost. From the research Mark Green, chief executive, and co-founder of Connexient, 30%-40% of the patients and visitors will get lost and waste their time in the hospital. This issue cause problems for the hospital at efficiency, experiences, and emotions for doctors, patients, and family member and friends.

Source: http://theweek.com/articles/761108/people-keep-getting-lost-hospitals-app-wants-stop-that

Goal

Control Efficiency of the Hospital

Improve Localization

Some people do not understand English very well or do not understand it at all. We would like to add this to break the language barrier between locals and doctors, and doctors to doctors.

Improve Emotion Experience

Hospital is a controlled environment. So things can be very stressful very easy. We would like to improve a way to reduce stress and make people calmer.
